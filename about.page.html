About Us

We at The Aura Ware are an online boutique that curates fashion. We have a store presence but since our retail space is the Internet; technology being our key strategy to gather everyone together. Powered and driven by a Team, the Aura Ware brand philosophy is all about asserting individuality and freedom. The Aura Ware is the Project of Aura Team.

We as a brand want to challenge the existing narrative of female fashion by rebelling against the norms of what is accepted and expected. The idea behind curating this brand is to nurture a culture of acceptance, creativity and diversity in fashion. Our outfits are fluid and open to interpretation of our Aura Ware. Aura Ware is not just a brand but also a state of mind; which is the vibe we wish to extend to all our public engagements as well: We will be immersive experiences that urge the customer to reexamine the existing attitude towards women and their sartorial preferences.

The Aura Ware hopes to translate the thought process of the flourishing millennial. We want them to feel engaged and understood which is why the tools we use and the strategies we employ are by the young, for the young. Our entire digital campaign has essentially been a series of impromptu sessions shot on the iPhone, in the city, thus proving, that passion and creativity will always trump big budgets and heavy names.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable products that enhance the lives of our customers. We are committed to sustainability, ethical sourcing, and exceptional customer service.

We strive to create a positive impact on our community and the environment through our business practices and partnerships.

Our goal is to be a trusted brand that customers can rely on for their everyday needs, while also making a difference in the world.

We believe in innovation, integrity, and inclusivity, and we are dedicated to fostering a culture of continuous improvement and growth.
